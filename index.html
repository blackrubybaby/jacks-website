Notable Achievements

  • 2023 George Benson Scholarship

    The George Benson Honorary Scholarship is an award presented annually at the Scottsdale Community College (SCC) Music Department Showcase. The scholarship is named after the 10-time Grammy Award Winner George Benson and is presented to a student in the SCC music program. In my pursuits of an Associate in Fine Arts at SCC, I had the honor of receiving it. For more information, click here.

  • 2022 Louis Armstrong Jazz Award

    The Louis Armstrong Jazz Award is an award that is given out to recognize and honor high school jazz musicians for their contributions to the genre. The award is named after the legendary jazz musician Louis Armstrong. The award is typically presented to musicians who have made significant contributions to the jazz community. The award honors one high school student per year.

  • AMEA Allstate 2nd Chair Tenor Saxophone 2022

    An award given out by the Arizona Music Educators Association (AMEA) to recognize and honor the top high school tenor saxophonists in the state of Arizona. The award is given out as part of the AMEA All-State High School Band, Orchestra, and Chorus program, which is designed to recognize and promote excellence in music education among high school students in Arizona. The award is typically given to students who have demonstrated exceptional skill and talent on the tenor saxophone, as well as dedication to their music development and a strong work ethic.

  • AMEA Regional Festival Participant 2019-2021

    Participation in the Arizona Music Educators Association (AMEA) Regional Festival on tenor saxophone is an opportunity for high school students in Arizona to showcase their musical talents and skills. To participate in the festival, students typically need to audition by performing a prepared piece of music and scales for a judge. The Regional Festival is a stepping stone to the All-State festival where the highest-rated high school performers are chosen to perform. It is an excellent opportunity for high school musicians in Arizona to gain recognition and to further develop their musical talents and skills.
